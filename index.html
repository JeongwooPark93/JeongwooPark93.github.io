About Us

We are the Four Monks of Mass. With a passion for Mongolian Folk music, we are in production for our 24th album, "Mongolian". Consisting of four soprano monks, this band is working to change the face of Mongolian Folk Music in monestaries around the world. Check out our hit single "Mongolian"

Meet Father Jeongwoo

Father Jeongwoo resides from deep in the forests of rural Illinois. Growing up in a hut, she found her passion for singing when she was asked to call her many dogs back to the house for supper. She joined the monks after her family left her at the gas station at the age of 3. Since then, she has taken the role as back-up to the back-up lead soprano singer, and plans on staying in that position until she dies.

Meet Father Luhee

Father Luhee, a Malibu native, is well-renowned for his soaring soprano range and facility. He joined the group in 2017 for their Chant-pionship win at the 1252th annual Chant-off where the group chanted for record 30 hours straight.
